# Gentle High Throughput Configuration

**Intent**: Move large volumes of fluid while keeping cavitation relatively mild.

**Key Changes**:
- Larger gap (1.5 mm) → lower velocity per channel
- Steeper nozzle angle (13°)
- More discs (28) for parallel flow

**Expected Behavior**:
- Easier to achieve high mass flow rates
- Lower shear rates → larger bubbles, less violent collapse on average
- Good for studies where you want lots of bubbles but controlled energy input

**Use Case**: Baseline comparisons, high-volume gas injection experiments, or when driving pressure is limited.
