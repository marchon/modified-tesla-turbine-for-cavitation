## Why These Links Matter for This Device

The entire platter flow conditioner project exists to give experimenters a **better, more repeatable “straw”** for the kinds of cavitation experiments Greenyer demonstrates in the videos above. By replacing crude bubbling or direct horn injection with a precision spiral + HVAT conditioned jet, the community can run cleaner, better-documented matrices and hopefully produce higher-quality data for the field.

If you build and test this device, please share your videos, photos, logs, and (especially) any anomalous results with the MFMP community and on LENR-Forum. That is the spirit in which it was designed.
